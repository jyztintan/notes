# Stack 

Abstract Data Type. Last-in-first-out data structure

Essentially a protected (Single) Linked List

All operations are O(1).

## Push

Add to top of stack

- Implemented by inserting element at head of the SLL, $O(1)$

## Pop

Remove from top of stack

- Implemented by removing element at head of the SLL, $O(1)$

## Peek

Look at the item top of stack

- Implemented by getting element at head (`peekFront()`) of the SLL, $O(1)$

## Applications

- Bracket Matching
- Postfix Calculator
